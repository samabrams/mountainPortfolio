About Me

A Little About Me

I grew up in Spartanburg, SC, the same place both my parents went to high school. I moved to Southern California in 2011 to attend Pomona College, where I received a Bachelor's in English in 2015. I now live in Montclair, CA with my best friends from college, my wonderful girlfriend, and my rats, Miles and Mingus. I'm happy to work anywhere in the Los Angeles or Orange County area. I can listen to audiobooks or podcasts on a long commute.

I’m driven by a passion for people, a commitment to growth, and a thirst for experience. I taught K-12 students for a year after graduating college, including an unexpectedly high amount of English learners, and I learned that understanding people and building positive relationships goes far beyond the language we exchange. I believe in the warmth of a relaxed smile, and I strive to share the love that can help everyone find it. I understand people, including myself, as works in progress, and I thrive on opportunities to learn and grow. As a developer, as a partner, as a friend, as a person, I know that I can always do more to become better, to understand more fully, and to contribute more positively to the lives I touch, and I welcome challenges to take steps forward.

I find great power in expression, so I care a great deal about the arts. Music is my chief passion. I sing everywhere I go, and I’ve casually created music in my free time for as long as I can remember. I spend several increasingly sore days every year among the hordes at concert festivals, and the thrill of live music still tends to leave me starry-eyed. I’ve been lucky to see many of my favorite artists (like Kanye West, Sufjan Stevens, LCD Soundsystem, and Bon Iver) in person, but I have many left to see (like Frank Ocean, Nicolas Jaar, Kendrick Lamar, and Fleet Foxes).

My love for expression combines with my love for language to yield a deep affinity for well-told stories. That affinity led me to an undergraduate focus on English Literature, and I especially loved studying modernist writers like T.S. Eliot, ee cummings, F. Scott Fitzgerald, and Ernest Hemingway. The same love drives my enjoyment of poets like Allen Ginsberg and Pablo Neruda, songwriters like Leonard Cohen and Bob Dylan, television writers like Vince Gilligan and David Simon, and game developers like Telltale or Naughty Dog.

The desire to grow pervades these passions, as well. I seek to hear stories that speak to communities apart from my own, to listen to music that challenges my preconceptions, to consume film and television that expands my experience and pushes me out of my comfort zone. I seek out information from outside my experience to help push me forward, and I’ve spent hours upon hours aggregating data into spreadsheets that help me optimize my free time.

I tend to unwind with sports, which have long provided a source of community for me. I grew up attending Carolina Panthers training camp in Spartanburg and watching every Atlanta Braves game on TV, and Death Valley in Clemson, SC might still be my favorite place in the world. These days, I bleed the red of Liverpool Football Club, and I keep up with the Charlotte Hornets now that they're back in town.
