About Me

A Little About Me

I grew up in Spartanburg, SC, the same place both my parents went to high school. I moved to Southern California in 2011 to attend Pomona College, where I received a Bachelor's in English in 2015. I now live in Montclair, CA with my best friends from college, my wonderful girlfriend, and my rats, Miles and Mingus. I'm happy to work anywhere in the Los Angeles or Orange County area. I can listen to audiobooks or podcasts on a long commute.

In my development, I tend toward the clean, simple, and fun, in terms of both design and code. I also love to push myself, and I'm looking to rise to a new challenge with every application I build.

I love the arts, and I'm especially passionate about music. I find a myriad of musical styles rewarding, but I listen to Kanye West, Bon Iver, Nicolas Jaar, Sufjan Stevens, Frank Ocean, Fleet Foxes and LCD Soundsystem more than most artists. I also love TV (especially Game of Thrones) and video games (especially FIFA). And, as an English Major, I of course love literature! The Unbearable Lightness of Being and Kafka on the Shore are two of my favorite books.

I'm also a huge sports fan. I love the Clemson Tigers (my mom went to Clemson), and I grew up attending Carolina Panthers training camp in Spartanburg and watching every Atlanta Braves game on TV. These days, I also devote a lot of my sports energy to Liverpool Football Club, and I keep up with the Charlotte Hornets now that they're back in town.
